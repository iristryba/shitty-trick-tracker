What can Shitty Track Tricker do for you?

Track your tricks

Finally landed the trick you've been working on for months? Imagine the satisfaction of finally checking that checkbox!

Plan your session

Do you know that feeling of arriving at the skatepark and not knowing what to work on? Plan your sessions ahead to make the most of them and learn new tricks faster!

Celebrate your progress

Feeling like you hit a plateau and you are not progressing anymore? Check back on your past progress, remember where you started and celebrate what you already achieved!

Why shitty?

Embrace the suck

The idea of this tracker I have had for about a year now. I even started working on an app to make it happen! But as always, I hit a point where it became too hard, too overwhelming, too... imperfect for my taste. And I stopped working on the project.

This time, I started yet another course on web development. But this time, I made up my mind to do things differently and focus on finishing projects. Finish them to the best of my ability, but imperfect and shitty!

The above also happens in the skatepark when we are working on our tricks. We get so worked up over not landing things perfectly, that we forget to have fun with the process and embrace the suck that is an inevitable part of learning. Let's be shitty at things together!
